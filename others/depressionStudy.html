Depression Study


Purpose of Study

To examine the effects of a probiotic supplement on symptoms of depression



Lab

Milev Laboratory

Who's Doing the Study

Caroline Wallace, PhD student, Centre for Neuroscience Studies, 613-544-4900 ext. 53325, caroline.wallace@queensu.ca




Location

Providence Care Hospital. Kingston, ON


Compensation Info

Participants will are reimbursed $50 per study visit and $50 per MRI completed



Benefits of Participating in Study

Participation in the study will help researchers understand how probiotics affect depressive symptopms



Inclusion/Exclusion Criteria

Inclusion: (1) Adults aged 18-65 in a current episode of depression
Exclusion: (1) Currently not taking any antidepressant medication




Participant Expectations

At study appointments participants will fill out questionaires and provide blood and urine samples. They will also undergo 2 MRIs and 3 polysomnograms.





Times Available:

Participants are recruited on a rolling basis and study visits are by appointment only. Please contact Caroline via the contact researcher button below and we can coordinate times
