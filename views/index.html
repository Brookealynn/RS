About Us

Human-based research is a fundamental pillar of progress in society. Research Stream was created to address inefficiencies in the research process so that researchers and participants can focus on advancing science and improving lives.

Research Stream is a platform that connects researchers and research participants, creating an initial point of contact, streamlining communication and increasing the efficiency of research management.

Frequently Asked Questions


How do I post my research study?

Fill out our online form with the details of your study, eligibility criteria and availability to meet with participants. Our team will work with you to present your study in a clear way to attract potential participants

How will people find my study?

Participants can browse all studies and search for studies more applicable to them through keyword searches or filtered browsing

How will I connect with potential participants?

Participants will be able to read the description of your study and if interested, they will request a time to meet with you based on the availability you have provided

How will I know if someone is eligible for my study?

When a prospective participant has requested a time to meet, you will be provided with the participant’s preferred method of contact allowing you to ask any specific questions you may have

Why should I participate in research studies?

Participating in research allows you to contribute to the advancement of science and healthcare by making an impact in areas that matter to you

Additionally, many studies may provide monetary compensation and/or valuable information about yourself which can be used to improve your life
